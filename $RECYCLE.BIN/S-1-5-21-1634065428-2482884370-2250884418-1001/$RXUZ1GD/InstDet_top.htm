Instructions With Machine Cycles and Flag Status

 Codes                   B/M/T         Machine Cycles     Flags
  DDD=Binary digits identifying   B=Bytes           F=Fetch with 4 T-states   S=Sign (D7)
      a destination reg           M=Machine cycles  S=Fetch with 6 T-states   Z=Zero (D6)
  SSS=Binary digits identifying   T=T-states        R=Memory read             Ac=Auxiliary carry (D4)
      a source reg                                  I=I/O read                P=Parity (D2)
                                                    W=Memory write            Cy=Carry (D0)
  Reg: B=000;C=001;D=010;E=011                      O=I/O write              
       H=100;L=101;A=111                            BS=Bus idle               x=Flag is modified
                                                                              0=Flag is cleared
  Rp:   BC=00;HL=10;DE=01;SP=11                                               1=Flag is set
                                                                          Blank=No change in flag state
